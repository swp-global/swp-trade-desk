How to Export from Your Broker

Step by step
Robinhood
1. Open Robinhood app or web
2. Go to Account → Statements
3. Select Tax Documents or History
4. Download as CSV
Webull
1. Log in to Webull desktop
2. Go to Orders → Order History
3. Click the export icon (top right)
4. Download CSV file
Tastytrade
1. Log in to Tastytrade
2. Go to History tab
3. Set your date range
4. Click Export → CSV
